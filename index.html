Privacy Policy for MyLife

Last Updated: January 5, 2026

Introduction

MyLife ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection and Storage

What We Collect

  • Local Data Only: All your data (to-dos, module information, vault documents, photos) is stored locally on your device using SwiftData and encrypted storage.
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information on our servers.
  • No Analytics: We do not use third-party analytics services.
  • No Advertising: We do not display ads or share data with advertisers.

Data You Provide

  • Task and reminder information
  • Module-specific information (budget items, health records, pet information, etc.)
  • Vault documents and photos (encrypted locally)
  • PIN/Biometric authentication data (stored securely in iOS Keychain)

How We Use Your Data

All data remains on your device and is used solely to:

  • Display your to-dos and reminders
  • Send local notifications for upcoming items
  • Provide encrypted storage for sensitive documents
  • Enable App Shortcuts and Widgets

Data Security

  • Vault Encryption: Sensitive documents in the Vault are encrypted using CryptoKit with AES-GCM
  • Biometric Security: We use Face ID/Touch ID through iOS Keychain for vault access
  • No Third-Party Access: Your data never leaves your device

Permissions

Camera

  • Purpose: Used for scanning documents into your Vault
  • When: Only when you explicitly use the "Scan Document" feature
  • Key: NSCameraUsageDescription

Photo Library

  • Purpose: Used to attach photos to vault documents
  • When: Only when you explicitly select "Add from Photos"
  • Key: NSPhotoLibraryUsageDescription

Notifications

  • Purpose: Send reminders for upcoming to-dos
  • When: Only after you grant permission and enable reminders
  • Control: Can be disabled in iOS Settings

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Delete Data: Uninstalling the app removes all data
  • Export: Use iOS Files app to access exported documents (if applicable)
  • Control: You have full control over all data through the app settings

Children's Privacy

MyLife does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences and operates entirely on-device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.
